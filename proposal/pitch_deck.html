Nobody's Solving This

× Doesn't scale

Millions of claims per day. Seconds per review. Human reviewers miss subtle edits and burn out fast.

Commercial ML

× Expensive & brittle

Proprietary, costly, and poor on mobile phone photos. Not designed for e-commerce fraud specifically.

BitMind (SN34)

× Wrong problem

Detects fully AI-generated images, NOT AI-assisted edits on real photos. Different problem entirely.

Key insight: The majority of refund fraud uses real photos with human edits — not AI generation.

How It Works

30% of tasks

Probe Tasks

Validator generates synthetic forgeries with known ground truth.

Provides an objective, manipulation-proof quality signal. Miners don't know which tasks are probes.

70% of tasks

Real Tasks

Merchant queries scored by weighted consensus among miners.

Higher-accuracy miners have more influence. Real commercial value delivered.

Score = 60% Probe Accuracy + 35% Consensus + 5% Speed

Commit-Reveal Protocol: Miners commit hashed answers before seeing others — no copying, no free-riding.
